## Navigation

:::note

The quickest way search blog posts is to click **Topics**

:::

⬇️ Scroll down to view blog posts
- ⬅️ The left is a list of blog posts. Click one to display it
- ➡️ Click an index of the selected blog post
- ↖️ Click **Topics** for blog post with tags. Click a tag for all blog posts with that tag
- Blog post tags are at the end of a post. Click a tag for all blog posts with that tag
